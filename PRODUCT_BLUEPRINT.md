# Product Blueprint

## Product promise
A disciplined Kalshi trading application that is high-volume but selective, singles-first, research-assisted, and continuously improving through audit.

## Trading principles
1. Confidence beats volume-for-volume's-sake
2. Singles are the primary profit engine
3. Combos are rare and only used when every leg is exceptionally strong
4. EV helps, but does not override weak projection or low confidence
5. Daily audit improves thresholds and selection quality over time

## MVP scope
- production-ready FastAPI backend
- dashboard
- background loops
- local market classification
- research note ingestion
- single-first ranking
- bankroll sizing
- daily audit

## Not in MVP yet
- external data vendors
- true portfolio optimizer
- distributed workers
- real-time websocket streaming
- model-driven projection engine
